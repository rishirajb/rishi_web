I am a Lecturer at the R. C. Bose Centre for Cryptology and Security. Previously, I was a post-doctoral researcher at the ARIC team of ENS-Lyon hosted by Damien Stehlé and Fabien Laguillaumie. I received my PhD from Indian Statistical Institute under the supervision of Bimal Roy.

My primary research area is Theoretical Cryptography. The goal of my research is to build the theoretical foundation of practical cryptosystems. I am also interested in Coding Theory and its interplay with Cryptography.
